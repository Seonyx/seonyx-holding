# Seonyx Holding Company Website - Project Overview

## Project Vision

Resurrect Seonyx (established 2010) as a professional holding company website showcasing multiple business divisions under one corporate umbrella.

## Historical Context

- **Original Purpose**: SEO services company (2010)
- **Name Origin**: "SEO" embedded in name for search visibility
- **Previous Platforms**: Mojoportal → Orchard CMS → Currently obsolete
- **Current Status**: Content migrated to techwrite.online, ready for complete rebuild

## Business Model

Seonyx operates as the parent holding company for:

1. **Techwrite** (techwrite.online) - Non-fiction writing and editorial services
2. **Literary Agency Division** - Representing science fiction authors (stable of pen names)
   - Primary: Maureen Avis (pen name)
   - Future: Additional authors to be added
3. **Inglesolar** (inglesolar.com) - Solar energy consultancy for Southern Spain
   - Focus: English buyers + Spanish renewable energy sellers
   - Status: Work in progress, no website yet
4. **Pixtracta** (pixtracta.com) - AI-powered real estate software
   - Technology: Image recognition for enhanced property search
   - Status: Development phase
5. **Homesonthemed** (homesonthemed.com) - Mediterranean property MLS
   - Integration: Works with Pixtracta
   - Status: Drawing board phase

## Website Purpose

- Professional corporate holding company presence
- Public-facing informational site
- Individual division showcases with dedicated sections
- Contact functionality (no exposed email addresses)
- Easy content management for non-technical updates

## Success Criteria

1. Clean, professional corporate aesthetic
2. Clear navigation between divisions
3. Easy content updates without coding
4. Mobile-responsive design
5. Contact form with anti-spam protection
6. Fast loading, minimal dependencies
7. GitHub-ready codebase with proper exclusions
